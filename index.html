Why You Must Visit Montauk State Park

In my opinion Montauk State park in Missouri is a must visit! Wether you like to Fish, Camp, or just want to spend some time in the outdoors this is the place to go!!

  • Camping
  • Fishing
  • Hiking

Montauk

Montauk State Park is one of Missouri's Trout parks. It has a vast history as an milling town turned into resort. The park is open all year around, with regulations differing from season to season. the park is appealing for a day trip, weekend get away, or even longer stays. For a group of friends or a family this is a great place for a retreat.

Camping in the park

Camping in the park is perfect for both the experienced and new campers. The campsites range from small natural sites, to sites big enough for mobile homes. Some have electric, and all have fire pits and grills for cooking. If camping isnt your thing, they also have a hotel and cabins for rent!

Trout Fishing

Trout fishing is the main attraction of Montauk and for great reasons. Rainbow trout are a fun fish. There are multiple ways to fish for a rainbow trout. The most popular way to fish for trout is "fly" fishing, where you use "flies" that mimic insects that the trout love. The other popular way to catch the fish is the use of a spinning real and bait.

Hiking and wildlife

Hiking may appeal to those who do not care to "wet a line". There are small trails that weave through the park and follow the river. The trails are perfect for beginner hikers as well for family walks. They are easily navigable and require no special equipment. There is also an abundence of wildife to view besides Rainbow trout. You are likely to see deer roaming the park. During certain months of the year you may even be lucky enough to spot an eagle
